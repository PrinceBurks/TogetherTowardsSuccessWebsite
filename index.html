OUR MISSION

Empower young families on their journey towards success.

We are building an educational care model for teen and young parents seeking a high school diploma or higher education while their children receive quality, structured academic support.

  • Serve underserved socioeconomic families with quality educational care
  • Support parent educational advancement with dependable child care
  • Provide clear child academic progress from start to end of year

Differentiation

We specialize in care for teen and young parents who are completing educational goals while raising children.

Target Family Profile

Families may be under federal poverty income guidelines, with scholarships also supporting eligible middle-income households.

Education Focus

We serve parents with no formal education through some college education, with pathways toward completion.
